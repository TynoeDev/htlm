About the Circle

Committed to Justice and Empowerment Through Theology

The Circle of Concerned African Women Theologians is a global network of scholars and activists dedicated to advancing gender justice and transforming societies through theological engagement. We provide a platform for African women to address critical issues and make a positive impact.

Learn More About Our Work

The Circle is a global network of African women scholars and activists committed to advancing gender justice and transforming societies through theological engagement.

Our work encompasses gender justice, cultural critique, biblical hermeneutics, interfaith dialogue, and advocacy & activism, all rooted in the African context.

You can support our work through donations, partnerships, volunteering, or by sharing our resources and publications within your network.

Membership is open to African women engaged in theology, religious studies, and related disciplines. Please visit our membership page for more details.
