About the Circle

A sisterhood of African Women Theologians

The Circle of Concerned African Women Theologians is a network of scholars and activists empowering African women through theological research, publications, and advocacy. We challenge patriarchal structures, promote gender justice, and foster social change within African communities and religious institutions.

Learn More About Our Work

The Circle is a network of African women theologians, scholars, and activists working to empower women, challenge patriarchal structures, and promote social justice through theological discourse.

We provide platforms for research, publication, and networking, fostering a supportive community that amplifies the voices and experiences of African women theologians.

The Circle addresses issues such as gender inequality, cultural critique, and the need for feminist interpretations of religious texts and traditions within African contexts.

You can support our work by becoming a member, donating, participating in our events, or sharing our resources with your network. Visit our contact page to get started.
